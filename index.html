Homage to ♡ Miss Lana Del Rey ♡

Lana Del Rey = A vintage aesthetic wrapped up in melancholia, beauty, and lore.

Welcome to the site that pays tribute to heaven's voice, Miss Lana Del Rey. Her music is noted for its stylized, cinematic quality; it explores themes of sadness, tragic romance, glamour, and melancholia, and it contains many references to pop culture, particularly 1950s and 1960s Americana. Her music stimulates both the heart and mind; transporting you to a hauntingly good time with her poetic words and her evocative, sweet voice and melody. She glamorizes pain by crafting tragic love stories that tell tales of sorrow and longing so beautifully and masterfully. She turns tragedy into beauty. Her style provokes us to take a trip back to déjà vu.

About Lana ♥
  • Born Elizabeth Woolridge Grant on June 21, 1985.
  • Del Rey was born and raised in Lake Placid, in upstate New York.
  • Del Rey graduated from Fordham with a Bachelor of Arts degree in philosophy in 2008.
  • Lana is a very talented songwriter, so it shouldn't come as a shock that she is also a poet.
  • In 2020, Lana announced she was releasing a poetry book titled 'Violet Bent Backwards Over the Grass'.
  • Del Rey says her current alias came to her during frequent visits to Spanish-speaking friends in Miami. She thought the name sounded glamorous and gorgeous in Spanish.
